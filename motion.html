Parallax Web Scroll

Goal

Create a webpage and animate this page to mimic a parallax scrolling feature of a web page. It should use multiple layers which seem to move at different speeds to create a sensation of depth and an interesting browsing experience.

Insight

The examples that I saw online about parallax web scrolling were all above ground scrolling into the ground. I wanted to do it a bit differently and use the sea. As you scroll further down the page you would go deeper in the depths of the water, discovering new things.

Storyboard

I sketched out the different levels of the waters for the scrolling effect.

Problems

I discovered a problem when animating in after effect. I would import all my illustrator layers but some of the last ones wouldn't show. I had to open a new illustrator file and create and save them individually to have them in after effects. I also had alot of trouble with making the scrolling effect look seamless but with lots of hours playing around with the 3D camera in AE I managed the look of scrolling down the page.
