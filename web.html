Freedom Dog Rescue

Freedom Dog Rescue is a non-profit charity which was founded here in Ottawa.

Web Design

Freedom Dog Rescue dedicates their time to rescuing abandoned dogs. They provide the dogs with a temporary home until they can be well-matched and find their forever home.

The original website was very outdated and bland. This organization's story is incredible. It was important to demostrate their story throughout the website.

CLIENT

Freedom Dog Rescue

DATE

April 2018

Branding

I began establishing a gray colour palette for the brand, since we want to represent sadness of abandoned dogs. However I wanted to bring some joy to the website as well, that's when I brought in the dark blue and the orange which worked well together.

Insight

My goal was for the website to be easy for the users to follow. I wanted to keep it very simple but still carry the information that is necessary without feeling overwhelmed.

Step 1: Research

The first step was to research the brand. I went on the website to find how it looks like and what I wanted to change. The website was already quite simple but I wanted to further enhance that but still make the website appealing.

Step 2: Planning

After I was done doing the research I moved on to moodboards. Moodboards helped me visualize the website I was envisioning. Helping find the right colour palettes and the right fonts. Along with which components I wanted to add. When this was all planned I moved to doing wireframes. This helped over see the layout of the website I wanted to create.
