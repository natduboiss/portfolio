Jumping Bean

Goal

To completely build an interactive prototype from scratch. From research reports, focus groups, paper prototypes and digital prototypes.

Insight

This was a team project and our goal was to build an app for a coffee company that would have locations around the world and have quick kiosks with simple drinks for customers to order and pick up at those kiosks.

Step 1: Research

The first step was to research the market. We research the market history, the conpetitors that we would go against, our target audience and demographic, etc. After our research plan was completely we created a questionnaire that we would use to interview our target audience with. After getting some answers with the interviews we created a research report with all of the data we collect.

Step 2: Planning & Testing

After the research was completed we moved on to sketch out our app prototype. Once the sketches were finalized we brought it in to Adobe XD and started building our interactive prototype. After these steps were completeted, it was time to test the app, which was done by doing a focus group.

Step 3: Final Result

With the feedback from our focus group we were able to fix the issues they documented for us and this was the final result of the interactive prototype. We also were picked by our teacher to showcase our project to some designs in the industry.
