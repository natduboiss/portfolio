Jumping Bean

An app to order simple drinks from Starbucks that connects to a kiosk that makes your drink.

UX/UI

Jumping Bean is an app that we developed as a team. The app connects to a kiosk owned by Starbucks for their customers to order simple drinks and skip lengthy lines.

We wanted the app to have the same feel as the Starbucks app, however before we started to design we needed to start by researching the competition in the coffee industry.
