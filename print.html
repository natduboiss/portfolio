Goal

The goal for this project was to completely re-design a logo for a company of my choice that's not currently designed very well.

Insight

My goal was to re-design the logo with a simple and modern approach. I wanted people to look at the logo and immediately understand the business.

Current Logo

I found slightly different logos for this business. When I first saw the logos I immediately started writing what needed changes. The banner needed to go it was very distracting. The type needed to be changed it was too hard to read. The overall logo needed to be more simple and more modern.

{% pattern sections/sketches-banner %}

Font

Choosing the correct font for this logo was challenging. I wanted a font that would be clean and not take the eye off the actual logo. I wanted to be simple but modern. That's why I decided to use the font Futura.

Final Logo

The original logo had the colour orange to it, which I didn't mind leaving. I was thinking about using a brown because the the meats they sell but the orange was a more better option because it makes the logo stand out.
