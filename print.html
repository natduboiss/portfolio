Smiles on Sparks

Goal

The goal for this project was to completely re-design a logo for a company.

Insight

I re-designed the logo with a simple and modern approach. I want people to look at the logo and immediately understand the business.

Current Logo

The current logo has a lot going on. It doesn't represent the company well, and looks outdated.

{% pattern sections/sketches-banner %}

Font

Choosing the correct font for this logo was challenging. I wanted a font that would be clean and not take the eye off the actual logo. I wanted to be simple but modern. That's why I decided to use the font Futura.

Final Logo

The final logo looks clean and modern. It represents the company very well.
