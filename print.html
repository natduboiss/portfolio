Smiles on Sparks

Smiles on Sparks is an Ottawa based dental office, that offers many dentistry services.

Branding

Smiles on Sparks is located in the heart of Downtown Ottawa. It offers a variety of services including general, cosmetic and restorative dentistry.

The primary problem we faced was that the original logo was hard to understand. It was important to create something that demonstrated a clean and modern logo for everyone to enjoy.

CLIENT

SMILES on Sparks

DATE

November 2019

Initial Logos

I began exploring different concepts: bold dark teeth, then lines coming together to form an image. None of them were working for me. When I presented these, we identified that the important ideas to convey were: make it simple and inviting for the patient's journey. These were big concepts that needed to be illustrated.

After a few iterations of logos I didn't like, I consulted with my team. I explained to them the importance of keeping the logo simple and inviting. They pointed out they enjoyed the idea of the flower design, it made it inviting. They advised me to keep the teeth more simple.

The Final Logo

After many iterations, we finally have the one! The team was on board, therefore I began developing stationery for the brand.
