Infograph

Goal

To create an infographic with a given subject. Learn how to design with a big amount of text and how to make graphs uniquely designed.

Insight

The subject that was given to me was The Growth of Allotments. I wanted the infographic to not feel too overcrowded with words that it's difficult to follow. However my biggest problem was to figure out a layout that worked with all the text I was given for the project.

{% pattern sections/sketches-banner %}

Step 2: Moodboard

I wanted this infograhic to implement an earthy colour palette. I also had the idea from one of the pictures on the moodboard about the infograph having the design of going deeper in the ground as you make your way down the page.

Step 3: Final Result

Even though my biggest problem was the layout and bringing all the text given together. I just kept rearranging the text and graphics around to figure out the best way to show the information.
