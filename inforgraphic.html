The Growth of Allotment

An infographic about the growth of allotment.

Infographic | Illustrations

For this project we were giving just the text and had to create an infographic based on the subject.

The challenge was to fit the information given on a 11x17 inches page with some illustrations. I knew I needed to start sketching my page layout and how I would seperate the information.

CLIENT

Algonquin College

DATE

April 2018

Moodboard

I developed and presented my moodboard to my team. From here, I established a style for the report that would be used for the inforgrahic.

The Final Product

After a bunch of sketching and consulting with my professor. I used the brown colours as sections for the information. I wanted the information to flow on the infographic and having those different brown colours really make the information consistent with each other but also give them sections.
